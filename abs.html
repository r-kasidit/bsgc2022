Presentation guidelines

Poster Presentation

  • All posters must be created in the “portrait” orientation (not “landscape”).
  • Mounting materials fitting to the boards (velcro tape or pins, etc.) will be provided.
  • All presenters have to finish setting up their posters before 12.00 pm on 15th December 2022. The poster can be removed after 5.00 pm on 16th December 2022
  • All presenters need to be at their own poster during the poster sessions. Please check the scheduling program to interact with judges and fellow participants

    • Oral Presentation

    Presentation preparation

  • Speaking time: 15 minutes overall (10 minutes for presentation and 5 minutes for Q&A)
  • File type: PowerPoint .pptx or .pdf
  • File name: Theme number_presenter’s_number_presenter’s name (ex. 1_001_Nutthanun)
  • Video format: .mp4

    • On the day of the presentation

  • Upload time: 48 h before the session starts, at least 1-2 hours before the presentation time at the registration desk
  • Please go to the presentation room 10 minutes early.
  • All files loaded to the congress computers will be automatically deleted at the end of each day.

    BSGC 2022 logo

    The logo for the upcoming “The 27th Biological Sciences Graduate Congress (BSGC 2022)” represents our lifestyle during the pandemic under the theme of ‘New Normal’. The color scheme depicts a new normal; ‘face mask’ is green, ‘alcohol’ is blue, and ‘ATK’ is white. The rain tree leaf is a symbol of Chulalongkorn University, and we gladly serve as the host of this congress. The four branches of leaf veins represent the main presentation themes in the congress.
